Our story

Family-Owned Auto Care in Dallas Since 1995

To provide honest, high-quality auto repair and inspection services that keep our community safe and moving forward.

Sabinas Automotive was founded in 1995 by the Sabinas family with a simple vision: treat every customer like a neighbor and every car like it’s your own. Starting as a small two-bay shop on Hampton Road, we built a reputation for integrity and technical excellence. Over the years, we’ve grown into a full-service auto repair center, but our core values remain unchanged – transparency, quality, and community.

Today, our team of ASE-certified technicians serves thousands of Dallas drivers each year. We invest in the latest diagnostic equipment and ongoing training to stay ahead of automotive advancements. Whether it’s a simple oil change or a complex engine repair, we approach every job with the same dedication. We’re proud to be a trusted part of the Dallas community and look forward to serving you and your family for years to come.
