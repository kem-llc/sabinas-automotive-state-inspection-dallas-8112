Texas State Inspection

Fast, thorough state inspections to keep you legal and safe.

Schedule this service

Overview

Texas requires annual safety and emissions inspections for most vehicles. At Sabinas, we perform the inspection quickly and accurately, checking brakes, tires, lights, emissions, and more. Our certified inspectors follow all state guidelines to ensure your car passes. We can also make minor adjustments on the spot to help you pass. No appointment needed – just drive in.

What's included

  • Quick turnaround – most inspections done in 30 minutes
  • Knowledgeable inspectors explain any failures
  • Minor repairs performed immediately to help you pass
  • Convenient location near major highways

How it works

1

Safety Check

We inspect brakes, tires, lights, horn, windshield, and seat belts.

2

Emissions Test

Using calibrated equipment, we measure exhaust emissions (if applicable).

3

Pass or Assist

If your car passes, you get your sticker. If not, we explain what's needed and can often fix it right away.

Common questions

Do I need an appointment for a state inspection?

No, walk-ins are welcome. However, calling ahead can minimize wait times.

What if my car fails inspection?

We'll provide a detailed report and can perform the necessary repairs. You have 15 days to get it re-inspected at no extra charge.
