AC & Heating System Service

Stay comfortable year-round with expert AC and heater repairs.

Overview

A malfunctioning AC or heater can make driving miserable. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and repair all climate control issues, from refrigerant leaks to faulty compressors and blend doors. We use specialized equipment to recharge your system to factory specs and ensure proper operation. Don't sweat through another Dallas summer – we'll get your cool air flowing.

What's included

  • Full system performance test included
  • Leak detection and repair using UV dye
  • Environmentally responsible refrigerant handling
  • Restores cabin comfort quickly

How it works

1

System Evaluation

We check pressures, temperatures, and inspect components for wear or damage.

2

Leak Detection

Using electronic and UV methods, we find even small leaks.

3

Repair & Recharge

We replace faulty parts and recharge refrigerant to manufacturer specifications.

Common questions

Why is my AC blowing warm air?

Common causes include low refrigerant, a failed compressor, or a clogged condenser. We'll diagnose the exact issue.

How often should AC be serviced?

We recommend an AC check every two years to catch small issues before they become costly.
